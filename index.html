Luke Gearty - Art Portfolio

A collection of photo manipulations that I have created. I have used stock photos from websites like Unsplash and Pexels in order to create these images. I also used 3D assets in a few of them.

Photo manipulation is a technique that utilizes software like Adobe Photoshop. It utilizes existing photographs to create new pieces of art.

I would compare it to making a collage, but trying to make the collage look like a cohesive piece of art. It takes influences from photography and digital painting.
